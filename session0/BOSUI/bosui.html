Brief Introduction
BOS UI supports role-based access control. There are three roles in BOS UI:
(1) Administrator, who is responsible for configuring the building.
(2) Operator, who is responsible for managing residents in the building and paying the electricity bill.
(3) Resident, who is living in the building and use the appliances on a daily basis.
In ESHL GUI, there is only one role, which is equivalent to the role of Resident in BOS UI.
