Brief Introduction
In this experiment, you will be presented with two user interfaces:
Building Operating System User Interface (BOS UI) and Energy Smart Home Lab Graphical User Interface (ESHL GUI)
Both are user interfaces for energy management in Smart Building. They provide residents with transparent information on energy consumption and energy generation in their building as well as control over their appliances.
BOS UI supports role-based access control. There are three roles in BOS UI: Administrator, who is responsible for configuring the building. Operator, who is responsible for managing residents in the building and paying the electricity bill. Resident, who is living in the building and use the appliances on a daily basis.
In ESHL GUI, there is only one role, which is equivalent to the role of residents in BOS UI.
